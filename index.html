welcome

One of Africa’s largest conferences on Web3 technology, presented in an innovative format

DAfCon23 is a fantastic opportunity for developers and blockchain professionals to come together, network, and stay up to date on the latest trends in the ecosystem. Africa has a reputation as one of the core sites of innovation in the web3 space — and DAfCon23 is here to celebrate this legacy and carry it forward.

DAfCon23 will draw the most influential figures, projects, and companies, spawning dozens of talks, masterclasses, and networking opportunities all around the four main tracks. There will also be an exhibition floor where guests can mingle with like-minded individuals.

Meet industry experts, government officials, funds and VCs, developers, entrepreneurs, and international organizations for important conversations, networking, business, and fun!

"As the crypto industry grows and disrupts adjacent industries, DeFi Africa strives to provide relevant information to investors, developers, and newcomers, allowing them to participate intelligently in the ecosystem. We will test ideas with industry partners to ensure that the DAfCon23 experience encourages learning, collaboration, networking, and creative content."

Sahabia Yakubu (Team lead, DeFi Africa)
